Why is my host/IP address (currently) blocked?

You are likely seeing this page because your host or IP address has recently been associated with activity that is causing AmiGO connections to drop for other users (including our monitoring bots).

For example, tens of thousands of expensive parallel queries in a few hours.

This status is temporary until our next release, the behavior has diminished for a period of time, or you have contacted us and we work something out. If you feel this is a mistake or would like to discuss server usage, please feel free to open a ticket on our helpdesk at http://help.geneontology.org/ .
